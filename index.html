Welcome! How to use this trainer.
  1. Select a practice mode. Random review presents cards in a truly random order, and you can reshuffle the deck at any time. Smart review adapts to your progress using a spaced-repetition (Leitner) system: cards you get wrong reappear sooner and cards you get right appear less often. Use the toggle at the top of the card to switch modes.
  2. Choose a Filter to pick what to practise (Prepositions, Numerals, Possessive, etc.).
  3. Read the sentence and type the correct form in the box.
  4. Press Check to mark your answer.
  5. Read the Why explanation, then click Next.
